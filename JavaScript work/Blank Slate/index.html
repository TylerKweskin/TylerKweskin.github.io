How to Play
1. Fill in the blank input on the Cue Card to complete the phrase/word, hoping to match with the others around the table.

2. Pass the device to the next person

3. Repeat steps 1-2 till everyone has put in ther word.

4. Keep playing till one player has reach a score of 25.
